# notion-sync

Connect your Nanobot to your Notion workspace.

## Usage
```
@Nanobot notion-sync --push --page "Daily Log" --content "Finished the prototype."
@Nanobot notion-sync --create --db "Tasks" --title "Review implementation plan"
@Nanobot notion-sync --pull --header "Project Notes"
```

## Features

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `Page Manager` | Create and update Notion pages with Markdown |
| `Database Integration` | Add entries to structured Notion databases |
| `Block Reader` | Retrieve specific information from Notion blocks |
| `Auto-Sync` | Periodically push heartbeat reports to Notion |

## Configuration
Requires `NOTION_TOKEN` and relevant `PAGE_ID` or `DATABASE_ID` in `config.json`.

## Philosophy
Keep your agent's digital life organized in the tools you already use. `notion-sync` ensures that the insights and tasks generated by your agent don't stay trapped in a chat history but live in your permanent knowledge base.
